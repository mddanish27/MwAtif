Atif Aslam was both Pakistan and India. is known for his vocal belting technique and his versatile genres. He was born on 12 March 1983 in Wazirabad, Punjab, Pakistan.

Pakistan, in 2008. He is married to Sara Bharwana, an educationist, and has three children..
